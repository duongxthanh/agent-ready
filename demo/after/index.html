Driftwood Coffee Roasters

Driftwood Coffee Roasters is a small-batch specialty roaster and café in Portland, Oregon. We source single-origin green coffee directly from farmers and roast it on-site every morning, so every cup is traceable from farm to café. Our menu rotates with the seasons and we publish tasting notes for each lot we release.

Our coffees

From bright, floral Ethiopian naturals to deep, chocolatey Latin American blends, every bag lists its origin, process, altitude, and roast date so you know exactly what you are brewing.

Visit the café

Find us downtown, open weekdays from 7am to 6pm and weekends from 8am to 4pm. We pour pour-overs, espresso, and a daily batch brew, and sell beans by the bag.
